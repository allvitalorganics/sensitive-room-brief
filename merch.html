Sensitive Room : Haus of K Merch Concepts
Vol I · Chapter 01 · Brooklyn

Merch concepts for the room.

A small first run of branded pieces for Chapter 01. The set carries the Sensitive Room: Haus of K lockup and one short tagline, kept to the program palette so every item reads as one family. These are concepts to direct production, not final artwork.

6 concept pieces Green + white print Black or cream garments 1 to 2 colors max
What the merch is for
Seed gifts for creators

A welcome piece for the cohort on the night of the workshop. It makes the room feel real, gives creators something to wear and shoot, and turns the audience into walking coverage for Haus of K.

Sponsor co-branding option

A clean back or sleeve area is left open so a sponsoring brand can be added as a small co-credit on select pieces (subject to the brand sponsorship terms). The Sensitive Room lockup stays primary.

The lockup and the palette
Sensitive Room : Haus of K

Wordmark with the double-o set in green is the primary mark across every piece. Tagline options that may sit under or beside it: "Break into beauty", "Chapter 01", "Haus of K". One mark plus one line per item, never both taglines at once.

These are concepts for production, not final artwork. Silhouettes and placements are directional; the maker sets exact print sizes, ink references (Pantone match to green 12713A and deep-green 0D572C), garment colorways (black or cream/natural), and stitch counts at quoting. Any sponsor co-credit is added only under the agreed brand sponsorship terms, with the Sensitive Room lockup kept primary. No em-dashes, palette locked to green, deep-green, white, and black.
