6 · Deal terms at a glance

What each side signs up for

Creator
  • Free to attend.
  • Makes one organic video per sponsoring brand (capped at 3), mandatory to participate.
  • First video within 14 days of the event, all within 30 days.
  • You own your content. Brand gets a 6-month organic repost license only.
  • Cohort creators are priority reach-outs for the brands; deals are non-exclusive, not guaranteed.
Brand
  • Sponsorship pricing is structured and shared by Nurilounge.
  • Paid 100% within 7 days of the workshop. Single payment.
  • One video from every creator assigned to it; with up to 50 creators, expect roughly 30 to 50 videos depending on the final sponsor count.
  • 2-deal follow-up minimum within 60 days, through the room.
  • 25% commission on deals booked through Sensitive Room, 12-month window.
  • A 2-minute intro slot in the room.
Partner split
  • Bucket 1 · sponsorship fees: split per the signed partnership agreement (partners only). Pricing structured by Nurilounge.
  • Bucket 2 · deal commission: of the 25% Sensitive Room commission, split per the signed partnership agreement (partners only). See the MOU.
  • Governing law: New York.
Keep it inside The exact split, the guarantee math, and the network-pool swap live ONLY in MOHO OS (the role-walled team app) and the signed MOU shared directly with Nurilounge, never on any hub page, the poster, the landing page, or any brand or creator surface. Creators see "free, one video per brand, priority shot at paid deals"; brands see "pricing by Nurilounge, video volume, 2-deal follow-up, 25% on deals we book for you."

8 · Tracking pointer

How we know it actually shipped

Each creator's videos (one per assigned brand, capped at 3) are logged in Discord #content-submissions, verified against the brief, and tracked to the timeline. Full schema, submission workflow, and the verification checklist live in the Ops & Content-Tracking Playbook.

Allocation engine, one line 1 brand to 1 video · 2 brands to 2 videos · 3 brands to 3 videos · 4 to 10 brands the Room picks 3 per creator, one video each. When there is more than one brand, Sensitive Room (not the creator) picks which brands a creator covers, balanced so every brand gets fair coverage.

10 · Top risks for a rushed pilot

What can break, and how we catch it

RiskMitigation
Too few creators by event day.Recruit every day, weekend push, Daesung's network as the magnet. Pilot floor is 20; if we are short, we still run with a smaller cohort rather than slip the date.
A brand drops out.Target 3 to 4 so one loss is survivable. Get verbal commits early, signatures by T-minus 2 days. The room runs even with a single brand.
Content slips past 30 days.14-day first-video gate surfaces slippage early in the tracker; reminders at +7 and +21 days from the event. All assigned videos verified in #content-submissions by +30 days.
No-show on the night.Confirm attendance in Discord at T-minus 1 day, send the address + time, and over-recruit against the floor to absorb drop-off.
FTC disclosure missed.The #ad / #sponsored rule is stated in the room at 0:28, written in the Participation Agreement, and checked at verification before a piece counts.
