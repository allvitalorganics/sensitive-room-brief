Target 4–5 brands so one loss is survivable. Verbal commit early, signed agreement by T-2 days. Room runs with a single brand if needed, don't delay for brand count.

14-day first-video gate surfaces slippage early. Justin pings at +7 and +21. Verify in #content-submissions before counting. Late = forfeit of that brand's license (written in the agreement).

MediumFTC disclosure missing on creator posts

Stated verbally at 0:31, written in Participation Agreement, checked at verification by Justin. Any post without #ad / #sponsored does not count. Creator must repost or add disclosure before it logs.

MediumBrand payment delayed past +7 days

Single 100% payment, no split schedule. Alex chases immediately after +7. Escalate to Aram if no response by +10 days.

LowRecording / footage failure

Two-camera setup if possible. Steph ensures footage backed up to two locations same night. Photographer as parallel backup for stills. The proof asset matters, don't lose the footage.

LowBrand intro runs long and kills the pace

Steph holds the timer, visible to the brand speaker. Flag at 1:45, wrap at 2:00. Daesung steps in if a brand won't stop. Pre-brief brand reps T-1 day on the hard cap.

LowConfidential terms leak to creators or brands

The exact split, the guarantee math, and the network-pool swap live ONLY in MOHO OS (the role-walled team app) and the signed MOU shared directly with Nurilounge, never on any hub page, the poster, the landing page, or any brand or creator surface. Public and partner-facing pages carry only the abstract fact that a partnership split exists.
