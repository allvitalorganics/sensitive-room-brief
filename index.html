What this is

The Sensitive Room is the doorway into K-beauty for creators who want in. One room, one evening: they meet the brands, learn how the space works from someone who lives in it, and walk out with a path to paid work.

Network

Direct access to K-beauty brands, the host, and a cohort of peers, in person, in one room.

Deals

A clear shot at paid brand partnerships, with the Room committed to chasing real follow-up work.

Knowledge

Daesung Seo guides the room on how K-beauty content actually performs and how to break in.

The pilot at a glance
The Workshop
  • When: dates announced soon
  • Where: Haus of K / Hana House, 345 Adams St, Brooklyn NY 11201
  • Host: Daesung Seo (2.5M)
  • Cohort: 20+ creators (cap 50), 3 to 4 brands
  • Format: brand intros, host talk, allocation handoff, filmed, networking
What a creator does
  • Free to join; one organic video per sponsoring brand, capped at 3 total
  • Each video: one in-feed short-form video (Reel or TikTok), brand handle + @sensitiveones.agency tagged, clear FTC #ad
  • First video within 14 days, all within 30 days
  • The Room assigns which brands each creator covers when there is more than one
  • A real shot at paid deals through the Room, as a priority reach-out
What a brand gets
  • One video from every creator assigned to you: roughly 30 to 50 videos with up to 50 creators, depending on final sponsor count
  • 6-month organic repost license on the content
  • A commitment to pursue 2 paid deals from the cohort within 60 days
  • Deals you book with our creators run through Sensitive Room
  • Pricing is structured and shared by Nurilounge
ConfidentialityCommission rates, the SOA:Nuri split, the network-pool swap, and guarantee math live only inside the partner-only documents. They are not printed on this page.
